History

World Tours (Pvt.) Ltd. has been providing excellent services to the Guests of Allah at minimum cost since its founding in 2003. Our success is due to the unwavering support of our valued clients, the pilgrims. Our Hajj Group has grown steadily each year without any advertising, a testament to our commitment to making the Hajj journey as smooth and comfortable as possible.

Our Group's pilgrims benefit from the 24-hour presence of our Directors and Religious scholars who provide reliable and detailed information throughout the journey. As a travel agency and Hajj and Umrah service provider, World Tours strives to build strong relationships with our customers, providing the best possible deals and employing highly skilled and qualified professionals backed by the latest technology and infrastructure.

We are constantly adapting to the changing environment and new standards in both Pakistan and Saudi Arabia to meet the growing service demands of Hajj and Umrah pilgrims. World Tours offers various packages to meet the varying requirements of our valued customers, and our committed staff ensures quality service from departure to arrival back in Pakistan. We leave no stone unturned in facilitating our esteemed pilgrims, allowing them to fulfill their religious obligations with ease and comfort.
